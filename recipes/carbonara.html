Fettuccine Carbonara

Description

This fettuccine carbonara is a delectable, mouth-watering pile of yummy goodness! I recommend a nice salad with it — that's all you will need for a complete meal.

Carbonara Ingredients

  • Olive oil: 5 teaspoons
  • Shallots: 4 diced
  • Bacon: 1 pound, cut into strips
  • Onion: 1 large, cut into thin strips
  • Garlic: 1 clove, chopped
  • Pasta: 1 (16 ounce) package dry fettuccine pasta
  • Parmesan cheese: ¾ cup shredded
  • Cream: ½ cup heavy
  • Egg yolks: 3
  • Salt and pepper: to taste
